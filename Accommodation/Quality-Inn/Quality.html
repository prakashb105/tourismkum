Welcome to Quality Inn Viha

Quality Inn Viha

Featuring a 24-hour front desk, a spa and massage center and a steam and sauna room, Quality Inn VIHA is located just 500-yards from Kumbakonam Bus Stand as well as Kumbakonam Railway Station. It also has 4 dining options. The property is located 65 mi from Tiruchirapalli International Airport. Navagraha Temples are 4.3 mi away and Maha Maham Tank is 0.6 mi away. Offering luggage storage, car rental and disabled guest facilities, Quality Inn VIHA also houses shops in the hotel, a terrace that provides views of the surrounding areas and a tour desk. Barbecue facilities are also available. Air-conditioned rooms feature a seating area and are equipped with an electronic safe, wardrobe, ironing facilities, electric kettle, flat-screen satellite TV and mini-bar. The private bathrooms contain complimentary toiletries. Thanmayi Restaurant serves delectable Vegetarian cuisine. Cloves Fine Dine Restaurant serves Indian, Chinese and Continental fare. Kafe Bar and the Black Lounge Bar serve refreshing alcoholic and nonalcoholic beverages. Room service options are also available.
