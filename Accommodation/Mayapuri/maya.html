Welcome to Hotel Mayapuri Amira

Hotel Mayapuri Amira

Featuring free WiFi and an outdoor swimming pool, Mayapuri Amira offers accommodations in Kumbakonam. Guests can enjoy the on-site bar. Each room has a flat-screen TV. You will find a kettle in the room. For your comfort, you will find bathrobes and slippers. You will find a 24-hour front desk at the property. The guests can approach the tour desk for the currency exchange and car rental services. The hotel also offers bike hire. The dry cleaning, ironing service and laundry facilities are available at a surcharge. Adi Kumbeswarar Temple is 2.6 mi from Mayapuri Amira, while Mahamaham Tank is 3.1 mi away. The nearest airport is Tiruchirapalli Airport, 44 mi from the property.
