Vinayaka Hotel

Hotel Vinayaga is located in Kumbakonam, famous for Hindu Temples. It offers a restaurant and cozy air-conditioned accommodations with a TV. Free Wi-Fi and parking are provided. The modern rooms come with neutral color tones and large windows that provide plenty of natural light. Guests enjoy the convenience of amenities that include a telephone and private bathroom with shower facilities. The restaurant serves a fine selection of traditional South Indian vegetarian specialties. In-room dining options are also possible with room service. With a 24-hour front desk, the hotel provides luggage storage and car rental services. Staff at the tour desk can assist with day trip planning and sightseeing arrangements. Hotel Vinayaga Kumbakonam is 1.2 mi from Ramanatha Swamy and Kashi Vishwanatha temples. It is a 2-minute walk from Kumbakonam Railway Station and 53 mi from Trichy Airport.
